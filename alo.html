Aloe Vera Tree Care Guide

Aloe Vera Tree

Aloe Vera is a succulent plant species that has numerous health benefits. It is known for its medicinal properties, particularly for skin care, and can also be used as an ornamental houseplant. Aloe Vera is low-maintenance and easy to care for, making it ideal for beginner gardeners.

Maintenance & Care

Here is some important information to help you take care of your Aloe Vera tree:

  • Watering: Aloe Vera needs water once every 3 weeks.
